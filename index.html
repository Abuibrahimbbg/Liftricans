The Project

At Liftricans, we aim to leverage the potential of open source to pave the way for aspiring Africans Tech Enthusiasts. Our platform brings together a diverse array of high-quality resources from external contributors, creating an inclusive and supportive community for practical learning and mentorship. Join us in constructing a cooperative future where every African possesses the necessary tools to contribute to the dynamic field of technology

Our Mission

To empower and uplift African youth through technology, providing free, accessible, and high-quality educational resources to equip them with the skills needed for success in the digital world

Our Vision

Africa where every young person has the opportunity to acquire digital skills and knowledge, enabling them to thrive in the modern tech-driven economy and contribute to the growth and development of their communities and the continent as a whole
